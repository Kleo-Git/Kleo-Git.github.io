# Project Overview
A project to create a working neural network from scratch.

# Purpose
To gain a full understanding of standard NN's by looking indepth at the underlying theory. By avoiding high-level libraries like PyTorch or TensorFlow this allowed me to implement forward and back passes manually and gain understanding in:
* **Matrix Calculus** Calculating gradients for weights and biases.
* **Activation Functions** Implementing Sigmoid, ReLU, Softmax and more functions.
* **Optimization** Stochastic gradient descent, Adagrad and Adam.
